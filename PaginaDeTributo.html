Hachikō

Faithful dog Hachikō

Life history:

  • Born on 10 November 1923, at a farm near the city of Ōdate, Akita Prefecture.
  • He was adopted by Hidesaburō Ueno, a professor at the Tokyo Imperial University, as his pet in1924.
  • During 1924 and 1925, he went to meet his owner at Shibuya Station.
  • Ueno died on 10 May 1924 but Hachikō continued to go to meet him for another almost nine years.
  • On 8 March 1935 he died, aged eleven, and will always be remembered as an incredibly faithful dog.
